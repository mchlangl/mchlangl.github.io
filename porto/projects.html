Tukang Kita (Web-based Application)

Description :
A web-based application that is made as a platform to connect people and service providers. My team consists 5 people (including me), and I take part in front-end, design, and reporting.

The backstory of the idea is our own personal experiences. It is quite hard sometimes to find some services like handyman, laundry, etc. It could take a lot of time and effort to find the convenience one. In other hand, we also thought about small/individual services that is struggling with promoting their services. That's why we made this app! ^^

Baemax (UI/UX Design)

Description :
A mobile-based application that used AI chatbot to be "a virtual friend" for users. By using Natural Language Processing, this chatbot could mimic an actual human and making a "real conversation" with user. This chatbot focused on motivates and give positive vibes (or quotes) to people by detecting the list of keywords that probably we would say when we're feeling down or not well.
